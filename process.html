Narrative

The website does a good job in many ways. It's packed with useful information, has attractive images that keep things interesting, and maintains a consistent design throughout. This makes it easy for users to find what they need and recognize the site wherever they encounter it.

However, there's always room for improvement. One major area to address is responsiveness. Right now, the website looks fantastic on desktops, but navigating it on phones or tablets can be a struggle. The menu doesn't shrink down as it should, and the layout doesn't adjust to smaller screens. This means a significant portion of users might miss out on the great content we offer.

Another area for improvement is readability. While the consistent design is a plus, some parts of the website lack color contrast. This makes the text difficult to read, which can be frustrating for visitors.

Finally, the website can feel a little cluttered at times. Simplifying the layout by reducing the amount of information on each page and streamlining the navigation menu would make it easier for users to find what they're looking for and improve the overall user experience.

Design Brief

The current website suffers from a lack of visual clarity and a structure that can be confusing for visitors. This makes it difficult for users to find the information they need and navigate the site effectively. To address these problems, Iâ€™m planning a comprehensive redesign that focuses on visual design, responsiveness, and a streamlined site structure.

One of the key improvements will be in the visual design. I'll be introducing a color palette that provides greater contrast, making text and important elements easier to read. Visual hierarchy will be established, guiding the user's eye to the most important information on each page.

Responsiveness is another crucial aspect of the redesign. With more and more people accessing websites on mobile devices, it's essential that the site adapts seamlessly to different screen sizes. This will ensure a positive user experience regardless of the device being used.

To improve the site structure, I'll be combining redundant pages and reducing the overall amount of information presented on each page. This will make the site less cluttered and easier to navigate. The content will be carefully curated to ensure it remains informative and relevant to the user's needs.

Finally, I'll be providing design templates for core pages such as Home, About Us, Contact, Services, and Resources. These templates will ensure a consistent user experience across the entire website, while still allowing for some flexibility in the way content is presented on each page.
