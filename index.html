We support individuals, families, friends, and communities impacted by myasthenia gravis

The Myasthenia Gravis Association (MGA) strives to build a supportive community for those affected by myasthenia gravis by increasing public awareness, providing educational opportunities, and fostering meaningful connections with one another.

What we do

The MGA works diligently to ensure individuals with MG are being supported and cared for through awareness efforts, education, member services and supporting research. Learn about the programs and services we provide here.
